Southern Gravy and Biscuits Recipe



Description:

There's nothing quite like the comfort of warm, flaky biscuits smothered in rich, creamy gravy. This classic Southern dish is a staple for breakfast or brunch, offering a perfect blend of textures and flavors. The biscuits are buttery and tender, while the gravy is savory and indulgent, made from pan drippings and a hint of spice. Itâ€™s a dish that brings a taste of Southern hospitality to your table, whether you're a seasoned cook or trying it for the first time.

Ingredients

For the Biscuits:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, chilled and cut into small cubes
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk, cold
  • 1 tablespoon melted butter (for brushing the tops)

For the Gravy:

  • 1/4 pound breakfast sausage (optional for sausage gravy)
  • 2 tablespoons bacon drippings or unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (optional, for a bit of heat)
